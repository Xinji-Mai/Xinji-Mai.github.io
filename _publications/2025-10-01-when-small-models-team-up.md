First-author work that defines the intellectual-property audit (IPR) task, proposes a Multi-Agent Audit Framework (MAAF) and an audit model (IPR-GPT), and shows a weak-expert ensemble surpassing larger LLMs. The system has been deployed in TikTok's IP-audit scenario.
